  • Why COURSES?

    We all hate Portal; Portal sucks. Perversely, HMC's version of Portal appears to suck the most, even though we are (by far) the most technically-oriented of the 5C's.

    COURSES is an attempt to show how much better Portal's course listings could be, if the students at HMC were allowed to design it.

    I mean, just look up---that's already an improvement, amirite?

  • How does it work?

    COURSES interacts with HMC's office of the registrar, as well as the heads of HMC's major programs to provide as up-to-date information about course offering and requirements as possible.

    It's important to realize that COURSES is not definitive; it does its best, but it won't always be up to date.

    Unfortunately, only Portal is guaranteed to be current.
